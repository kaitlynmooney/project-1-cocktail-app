Dirty Martini

Ingredients

  • 2oz gin
  • .75oz olive brine
  • .25oz dry vermouth

Recipe

  1. Add ice and ingredients to a cocktail shaker
  2. Shake cocktail
  3. Strain and pour into a chilled martini glass
  4. Garnish with 3 skewered olives
